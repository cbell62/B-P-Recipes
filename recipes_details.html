Egg Manchurian

An egg is the incredible source of protein and is counted as one of the most nutritious foods in the world. It's such a versatile ingredient that you can make anything with it, be it a dessert of breakfast! Here's a recipe that will be loved by all the eggetarians - Egg Manchurian. Thus Manchurian recipe is made just like the traditional dish, the only difference is the main ingredient used in it. he ingredients used in the preparation of this appetizer are easily available. If you love this egg recipe, just follow the steps carefully and prepare it for your loved ones.

  • Rating :

  • Time : 30 Mins

  • Category : Appetizer

  • Tags : Dinner, Appetizer, Egg

Ingredients of Egg Manchurian

2 onion

1/2 cup corn flour

refined oil as required

1/2 tablespoon garlic paste

2 green chilli

1/2 tablespoon ginger paste

1/2 cup rice flour

salt as required

4 egg

1 spring onions

1/2 teaspoon soy sauce

1 teaspoon red chilli powder

1 tablespoon coriander leaves

1 tablespoon red chilli sauce

How to make Egg Manchurian

Step 1: Chop the onion and green chillies

To prepare this tasty appetizer recipe, first, peel and slice the onions neatly using a chopping board. Also, wash and finely chop the green chilles and spring onions.

Step 2: Prepare the rice and cornflour batter

Then, take a medium-sized bowl and add rice flour along with most of the cornflour in it. Also, add salt and water as required to make a batter. Then, drop eggs in the bowl gently to coat them with the prepared batter.

Step 3: Fry the boiled eggs

Next, take a medium-sized deep bottomed pan and place it on medium flame. Then, pour some and oil in it. Once the oil has heated, pick 1 egg from the bowl and place it in the pan. Fry it until light brown. Similarly, do the same with remaining eggs. Transfer to an absorbent paper after frying and keep aside for a while.

Step 4: Prepare the masala or gravy for Manchurian

Now, in the remaining oil of the same pan, add garlic paste, ginger paste, red chilli powder, red chilli sauce and saute it on low flame for few minutes. Then, add a little amount of soy sauce in it along with salt as per taste. Then, add spring onions along with remaining cornflour and saute well. After that, add a little amount of water and mix properly to form a rich gravy.

Step 5: Mix fried eggs in the gravy and gently coat the masala over it, serve hot

Lastly, transfer the fried eggs (step 3) in the prepared gravy (step 4) and stir gently. Then, cover the pan with a lid and cook for a few minutes. Once cooked, transfer it to a serving dish and garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot.
