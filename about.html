About Us

B&P Recipes
Never Disappoint

We are B&P Recipes. We are based out of Charlotte, NC and are cooking enthusiasts. At B&P Recipes, you can do more than you ever imagined. You can view recipes for a variety of dishes that take 30 minutes or less to make. In addition, these recipes can be used by someone who has never cooked before to someone who is an expert in cooking. Best of all, if you wish to give feedback or share your own favorite recipes to be added to the site, you can do us on the contact page by filling out the form. You can do all this and more. Ultimately, our goal is to provide a platform for you to share recipes, to find new recipes to cook, and to become a better cook!
