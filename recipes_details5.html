Buffalo Chicken Enchiladas

Stuff corn tortillas with a cheesy Buffalo chicken filling for a mashup you'll crave again and again. It's an easy weeknight winner, thanks to rotisserie chicken.

  • Rating :

  • Time : 30 Mins

  • Category : Appetizer

  • Tags :Appetizer, Enchilada, Gluten-Free

Ingredients of Buffalo Chicken Enchilada

3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, plus more for greasing the pan

4 cups shredded rotisserie chicken

8 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature

2 cups shredded Cheddar

1 cup hot sauce, plus more for serving, such as Frank's

1 bunch scallions, thinly sliced, white and green parts separated

1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

16 corn tortillas

2 tablespoons crumbled blue cheese

2 tablespoons blue cheese dressing

How to make Buffalo Chicken Enchilada

Step 1: Preheat Oven and Prep Work

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Butter a 9-by-13-inch baking dish.

Step 2: Mixing and Stiring

Then, mix the chicken, cream cheese, 1 cup of the Cheddar, 1/3 cup of the hot sauce, white parts of the scallions and cumin in a large bowl until well combined. Stir together the butter, remaining 2/3 cup hot sauce and 3 tablespoons water in a medium bowl.

Step 3: Tortillas Cooking

Next, microwave the tortillas in batches until warm, softened and foldable, about 30 seconds. Keep warm between damp paper towels.

Step 4: Baking

Now, Spoon a portion of the chicken mixture down the middle of each tortilla and roll up. Place them side by side, seam-side down, in the prepared pan. Pour the hot sauce mixture over the tortillas. Sprinkle with the remaining 1 cup Cheddar and the blue cheese and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, 15 to 17 minutes.

Step 5: Final Touches

Then, drizzle the blue cheese dressing over the enchiladas and sprinkle with the scallion greens

Step 6: Serve

Serve with more hot sauce.
